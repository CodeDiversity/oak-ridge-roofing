# Oak Ridge Roofing Co.

## What This Is

A 5-page Astro.js demo site for Oak Ridge Roofing Co., a local roofing contractor. The site showcases roofing services, roof repair, storm damage, gutters, and inspections — positioning the company as a premium local option worth $3,500–$5,000 website investment. Built by Page and Pixel as a proof-of-concept for high-ticket local service websites.

## Core Value

A demo site that converts site visitors into inspection leads via mobile-first UX, trust signals, and a friction-free contact form.

## Requirements

### Active

- [ ] 5-page Astro site: Home, Roofing Services, Roof Repair, Storm Damage, Contact / Free Roof Inspection
- [ ] Homepage: Hero with CTA "Get a Free Roof Inspection", trust bar (licensed, insured, local, emergency repairs), services cards, before/after placeholder, reviews, service areas, FAQ, contact form
- [ ] Mobile-first, click-to-call on all pages
- [ ] Fast, accessible, production-ready Astro build
- [ ] Portfolio-quality design that justifies $3,500–$5,000 price point

### Out of Scope

- CMS / content management
- Blog or news section
- AI chat widget (planned as future add-on)
- E-commerce or quote calculator
- Multi-language support

## Context

- **Client:** Oak Ridge Roofing Co. — local roofer, high-ticket jobs (storm damage, full replacements)
- **Goal:** Demo site that impresses prospects, not a live client build
- **Design company:** Page and Pixel
- **Competitive context:** Most roofer sites are slow, generic, or DIY. This needs to stand out with speed, visual polish, and conversion UX.
- **AI add-on hook:** "Ask about roof repair, storm damage, financing, service area, or book an inspection" — keep this architecture extensible for future AI integration

## Constraints

- **Tech stack:** Astro.js — static-first, fast by default, easy to hand off
- **Pages:** 5 total (Home, Roofing Services, Roof Repair, Storm Damage, Contact)
- **Performance:** Sub-2s load, Lighthouse 90+ on all metrics
- **Mobile:** Click-to-call on every page, optimized for homeowners on phones after storm damage

## Key Decisions

| Decision | Rationale | Outcome |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Astro.js framework | Static-first, fast, clean hand-off, ideal for local service sites | ✓ Good |
| 5-page structure | Home + 3 service pages + Contact — clear journey, focused conversion | ✓ Good |
| Design by Page and Pixel | Brand attribution in footer, portfolio-quality presentation | ✓ Good |
